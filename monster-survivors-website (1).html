How to Play

Monster Survivors is an intense action survival game where your goal is to survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. Here's how to play:

Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks automatically
  • Press Space to use special abilities when available
  • Collect experience gems to level up

Tips for Success

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize leveling up early to gain an advantage
  • Choose upgrades that complement your playstyle
  • Watch for special monster types that drop better rewards

Challenge yourself to beat your previous records, and compete with friends to see who can survive the longest!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game inspired by classic arcade experiences. Developed with a focus on accessibility and depth, the game offers simple controls but deep strategic choices.

With its roguelike elements, every playthrough is different as you unlock new weapons, abilities, and face increasingly challenging enemy types. The game features a unique progression system that rewards both skill and persistence.

Whether you have five minutes or an hour to play, Monster Survivors offers an engaging experience that will keep you coming back for "just one more run."
